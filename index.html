CHEAT CODES & DEEP KNOWLEDGE
गुरु-उपदेश आधारित
Ashtakoot Milan is your psychology, your energy, and your karma — three things mixed together. This is the first stage of matchmaking, not the last. People who stop here make mistakes.
🔑 The 8 Koots — Sequence & Points
1. Varna — 1 pt
Spiritual layer, energy element, psychological archetype
2. Vashya — 2 pts
Control, dominance, attraction in relationship
3. Tara — 3 pts
Nakshatra energy, health, luck & destiny
4. Yoni — 4 pts
Biological behavior, physical love expression
5. Graha Maitri — 5 pts
Emotional & mental compatibility, friendship
6. Gana — 6 pts
Nature, temperament, response pattern
7. Bhakut — 7 pts
Life perspective, karmic struggles, luck flow
8. Nadi — 8 pts
Prana energy, progeny health, genetic compatibility
Minimum 18 Gunas required to be eligible for marriage. 18–32 is the recommended range. Below 18 = usually rejected. Above 32 = also sometimes cautioned against by some traditions!
The entire Ashtakoot Milan is based on Moon (Chandra Rashi) and Nakshatra. Lagna/Ascendant is NOT used here. That is why it is incomplete alone — your Moon being good doesn't mean Venus is good too.
💡 Why High Gunas ≠ Good Marriage
I have matched couples with just 4 gunas who are living beautifully. And I have seen 32-guna couples fighting every day. The karmic relationship (कार्मिक संबंध) between two people is far more important than the mechanical guna count.

When you look at couples with fewer gunas whose marriages are working — look carefully. You will find their karmic relationship is very strong. The ashtakoot system shows you Moon-level compatibility. But marriage needs Jupiter, Venus, Mars, Saturn — all planets' chemistry together.

I have matched a couple with 121 gunas (total system, not just ashtakoot). They are doing great. My own cousin had only 9 gunas matched. I pushed the family and got the marriage done. 6–7 years, everything is wonderful.
🔶 Varna — 1 Point | Spiritual Layer & Energy Element
Brahmin (Jal / Water)
Rashis: 4 Karka, 8 Vrishchika, 12 Meena
Knowledge + spirituality + emotions + adaptability
Kshatriya (Agni / Fire)
Rashis: 1 Mesha, 5 Simha, 9 Dhanu
Leadership + power + courage + decisiveness
Vaishya (Prithvi / Earth)
Rashis: 2 Vrishabha, 6 Kanya, 10 Makara
Practical + diplomatic + gets work done politely
Shudra (Vayu / Air)
Rashis: 3 Mithuna, 7 Tula, 11 Kumbha
Social + networker + service + compassionate
Brahmin doesn't mean they know the Vedas. It means their social circle, their surroundings, their knowledge level — whatever fits their world. Similarly Kshatriya's power doesn't mean physical strength — it means how long you can hold your ground in adversity.
Groom's Varna must be ≥ Bride's Varna for 1 point. If equal = 1 pt. If groom's is higher = 1 pt. If bride's varna is higher = 0 pts.
Jal (Brahmin) + Agni (Kshatriya): Agni boils Jal and turns it to steam → the Brahmin partner loses their identity completely. This is identity loss (आइडेंटिटी लॉस).
Vaishya's nature — they deal with everyone politely, always focused on getting their work done. Even if they curse you behind your back, in front they'll be sweet. That is their energy. And Shudra (Vayu) — always moving, always connecting, very high social drive, deep empathy — will give to a beggar on the street without thinking.
🔷 Vashya — 2 Points | Control, Dominance & Attraction

Vashya tells you who will be dominant in the relationship and what kind of freedom each partner needs.

Manushya (Dwipad / 2-legged)
Mithuna, Kanya, Tula, Dhanu (0–15°), Kumbha
Chatushpad (4-legged)
Mesha, Vrishabha, Dhanu (15–30°), Makara
Jalchar (Aquatic)
Karka, Meena, Makara (15–30°)
Vanchar (Forest)
Simha (Leo)
Keet (Insect)
Vrishchika (Scorpio)
Leo (Simha) + Pisces (Meena): Leo is authoritative, Pisces is emotional. Leo will dominate Pisces completely. That is what we called "Lalita Pawar" — the famous domineering mother-in-law character!

Scoring: Same type = 2 pts | Compatible types = 1 pt | Incompatible = 0 pts

⭐ Tara — 3 Points | Nakshatra Energy, Health & Destiny
Tara tells you about the energy compatibility of nakshatra — whose luck (भाग्य) and energy are complementing each other or not. Sometimes your luck is very strong but you're not ready to work — even luck can only carry you so far.
How to calculate:
Step 1: Count from Boy's nakshatra → Girl's nakshatra (inclusive). Divide by 9. Note remainder.
Step 2: Count from Girl's nakshatra → Boy's nakshatra (inclusive). Divide by 9. Note remainder.
Remainder 3, 5, or 7 = INAUSPICIOUS (अशुभ) in that direction.
Both good = 3 pts | One good = 1.5 pts | Both bad = 0 pts
Distance Rule (girl's nakshatra → boy's nakshatra):
Distance up to 9 nakshatras = Ashubha (bad) | 10–18 = Madhyam (moderate) | 18+ = Shubha (good)
Example: Bride=Ashwini, Groom=Jyeshtha (18th from Ashwini) = Madhyam. Mula (19th) = Shubha.
If Girl's nakshatra to Boy's nakshatra distance is only 2 (next nakshatra) — this is considered very inauspicious (बहुत अशुभ).
🐾 Yoni — 4 Points | Biological Behavior & Love Expression
Yoni is not just sexual compatibility. It's your biological behavior — your organic reaction. How do you express physical affection? Is physical touch important to you at all? What is your love language in the physical sense?
4 pts — Sama Yoni
Both the same animal — identical energy, perfect physical understanding
3 pts — Mitra Yoni
Friendly animals — compatible physical expression
2 pts — Sama (Neutral)
Neither friend nor enemy — average compatibility
1 pt — Shatru
Enemy animals — physical tension and discord
0 pts — Ati-Shatru
Mortal enemies — major physical incompatibility
7 Ati-Shatru (Mortal Enemy) Pairs: Ashwa↔Mahisha | Gaja↔Simha | Mesha↔Vanara | Sarpa↔Nakula | Shwan↔Mriga | Marjara↔Mushaka | Go↔Vyaghra
Ashwa (Horse) energy = completely free. "Don't bind me." Gaja (Elephant) = very intelligent, mature, stable, slightly serious. That ek thaharav (stability) will be in their love. Horse + Elephant = horse keeps running, elephant stays firm — they will exhaust each other.
Yoni compatibility is given great importance (बहुत ज़्यादा importance) in Shudra community marriages. In Brahmin tradition, Bhakut and Nadi are considered more critical.
🪐 Graha Maitri — 5 Points | Emotional & Mental Compatibility
Graha Maitri shows your emotional compatibility — how you are both emotionally driven, and whether those drivers are compatible. How can there be friendship without emotional compatibility?

Check the friendship between the Rashi Lords (lords of their Moon signs — not Nakshatra lords).

5 pts — Same Lord
Both rashis have the same ruling planet
5 pts — Mutual Friends
A is friend of B AND B is friend of A
4 pts — One-way Friend
A is friend of B, but B is neutral to A
3 pts — Both Neutral
e.g., Mercury + Saturn (both neutral to each other)
1 pt — One Enemy
One is friendly but other is enemy
0 pts — Both Enemies
e.g., Sun (Leo) + Saturn (Capricorn/Aquarius) = worst!
Sun's friends: Moon, Mars, Jupiter | Moon's friends: Sun, Mercury
Mars's friends: Sun, Moon, Jupiter | Mercury's friends: Sun, Venus
Jupiter's friends: Sun, Moon, Mars | Venus's friends: Mercury, Saturn
Saturn's friends: Mercury, Venus
Sometimes you need complementary energy. Sometimes opposing energy. Sometimes you need someone who can suppress (रोक सके) one energy so the other can grow. You need to see the chart to decide what energy this person actually needs.
🌀 Gana — 6 Points | Nature, Temperament & Response Pattern
Gana tells you: what kind of being are you? Very aggressive, mature, stable, composed? What is your response pattern? This is about how you react to situations in the relationship.
Deva Gana (9 nakshatras)
Ashwini, Mrigashira, Punarvasu, Pushya, Hasta, Swati, Anuradha, Shravana, Revati
Saumya (calm), composed, diplomatic
Manushya Gana (9 nakshatras)
Bharani, Rohini, Ardra, Purva Phalguni, Uttara Phalguni, Purva Ashadha, Uttara Ashadha, Purva Bhadrapada, Uttara Bhadrapada
Balanced — works with both Deva & Rakshasa
Rakshasa Gana (9 nakshatras)
Krittika, Ashlesha, Magha, Chitra, Vishakha, Jyeshtha, Mula, Dhanishtha, Shatabhisha
Aggressive response, fiery, intense nature
6 pts — Same Gana
Any two of the same gana (even both Rakshasa = 6!)
5 pts — Deva + Manushya
If Var (groom) = Deva, Vadhu (bride) = Manushya → 6
If reversed (bride=Deva, groom=Manushya) → 5
1 pt — Manushya + Rakshasa
Some tension but workable
0 pts — Deva + Rakshasa
Complete temperament clash (worst = if bride is Rakshasa)
Two Rakshasa gana people marrying each other = 6 points! They will fight each other brilliantly — but at least they are equal! They understand each other's aggression.
♐ Bhakut — 7 Points | Life Perspective, Karmic Struggle & Luck
Bhakut shows your life perspective — where your karma is struggling and where it flows smoothly. Is your luck highway smooth or full of speed breakers and zigzags? This koot is given MOST importance in Brahmin community of North India. In South India, Nadi gets more importance.
Inauspicious Rashi Angle Combinations (give 0 pts): 2–12 | 5–9 | 6–8
2–12 (द्वादश दोष)
Financial difficulties, wealth conflicts
5–9 (नव-पंचम दोष)
Children-related difficulties (संतान दोष)
6–8 (षडाष्टक दोष)
Disease and death fear, illness in family
Cheat Code 1 — Same Rashi: If both have the same rashi = Shubha! BUT nakshatra must be different. If same nakshatra, then charan/pada must be different. Both same charan = bad.
Cheat Code 2 — Natural Friends: 2–12 dosha is CANCELLED if both rashis are naisargik mitra (natural friends). Friend pairs: Vrishabha–Mithuna, Karka–Simha, Kanya–Tula, Vrishchika–Dhanu, Makara–Kumbha, Meena–Mesha
Cheat Code 3 — Same Lord Pairs: Bhakut dosha cancelled for same-lord rashi pairs: Aries↔Scorpio (Mars), Taurus↔Libra (Venus), Gemini↔Virgo (Mercury), Sagittarius↔Pisces (Jupiter), Capricorn↔Aquarius (Saturn)
Cheat Code 4 — Balance with other koots: If Vashya AND Nadi are both good, Bhakut dosha can be balanced. Also: if rashi lords are friends, OR nadi milian is pure + tara is shubha = ignore bhakut dosha.
Daridra Yoga (Poverty Yoga): Certain 4–10 combinations bring financial hardship: Vrishabha–Simha | Karka–Tula | Kanya–Dhanu | Vrishchika–Kumbha | Makara–Mesha | Meena–Mithuna
In the 5–9 direction: if groom's rashi is 5th from bride's = acceptable. But if bride's rashi is 5th from groom's = problems with progeny. Direction matters!
🌊 Nadi — 8 Points | Prana Energy, Progeny & Constitution
Nadi is your prana energy — your life force. From this same prana energy, your progeny (संतति) is born. If both partners have the same prana energy, the offspring's energy may be weak. That is why same-nadi marriage was forbidden in older times.
Aadi Nadi (Vata)
Ashwini, Ardra, Punarvasu, Uttara Phalguni, Hasta, Jyeshtha, Mula, Shatabhisha, Purva Bhadrapada
Madhya Nadi (Pitta)
Bharani, Mrigashira, Pushya, Purva Phalguni, Chitra, Anuradha, Purva Ashadha, Dhanishtha, Uttara Bhadrapada
Antya Nadi (Kapha)
Krittika, Rohini, Ashlesha, Magha, Swati, Vishakha, Uttara Ashadha, Shravana, Revati

Different Nadis = 8 pts (no dosha) | Same Nadi = 0 pts (Nadi Dosha)

Cheat Code 1: Same Rashi, DIFFERENT Nakshatra → Nadi Dosha CANCELLED. Example: Both Vrishchika — Bride=Anuradha (Madhya), Groom=Jyeshtha (Aadi) → Different nadis anyway. OR if both same nadi but different nakshatra, dosha still cancelled.
Cheat Code 2: Same Nakshatra, DIFFERENT Charan/Pada → Nadi Dosha CANCELLED. Example: Both have Ardra nakshatra, but bride in Charan 1 and groom in Charan 3 → No dosha.
Cheat Code 3: Split Nakshatra across two Rashis → Nadi Dosha CANCELLED. Split nakshatras: Krittika (Mesha/Vrishabha), Mrigashira (Vrishabha/Mithuna), Vishakha (Tula/Vrishchika), Purvashadha/Uttarashadha boundary, etc.
Nadi also tells you much more — it reveals your Ishta Devata (personal deity), the type of Mantra that works for you, where you will find Mukti (liberation), and your Ayurvedic Prakriti (Vata/Pitta/Kapha constitution). This is very sacred knowledge. I will tell you more about this when the time comes.
🔬 Advanced Matching — Beyond Ashtakoot (Stage 2 & 3)
Ashtakoot is Stage 1. People who stop here are making a big mistake. A complete match requires at least three stages.
Stage 1 — Ashtakoot
Moon + Nakshatra based. 36 gunas. This sheet covers this stage. Minimum 18 gunas.
Stage 2 — D1 + D9 Analysis
Analyze Lagna chart (D1) + Navamsha (D9) for both bride and groom. Check 7th, 4th, 2nd, 12th, 5th, 9th, 8th houses.
Stage 3 — Planetary Chemistry
Venus–Mars chemistry between the two charts. Girl's Mars nakshatra compatibility with Boy's Venus. Karmic relationship depth. Current + upcoming Dasha compatibility.
⚠️ Vadhavya Yoga & Vidhur Yoga
Check Vadhavya Yoga (widow yoga — वैधव्य योग) in the girl's chart. Check Vidhur Yoga (widower yoga — विधुर योग) in the boy's chart. If the girl has a strong Vadhavya Yoga, match her with a boy who has a Vidhur Yoga — the two doshas neutralize each other!

Rotate the 7th house of each person to lagna and examine: 7th from 7th, 5th from 7th, 4th from 7th, 12th from 7th. This confirms or weakens the intensity of the yoga.

📖 Jataka Parijata — Afflicted House Rule
If the same house is heavily afflicted in BOTH charts — they actually neutralize each other! For example, if both have the 2nd house badly afflicted — that is actually fine for them together. Counter-intuitive but this is the classical rule from Jataka Parijata.

Note: This rule applies to house afflictions, not to planets. With planets, we look for complementary/balancing energies.

💑 Love Marriages — A Practical Note
When a love couple comes to me and asks for matchmaking, I always ask first: "If the gunas don't match, will you still marry?" If they say yes, I say — then why did you come to me? There's no point in doing the matching if the decision is already made.

Some couples also come asking to "fix" their chart — even asking me to change the numbers to make it match. This is not how Jyotish works.
